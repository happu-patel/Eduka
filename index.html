About Us

Welcome To Noble High School

Located in the serene environment of Gadwal, Jogulamba Gadwal District, Noble High School is a CBSE-affiliated English medium institution offering Pre-Primary to High School education. Established in 2010, we are committed to shaping confident, creative, and character-rich individuals.

Nurturing young minds with conceptual clarity and all-round development in creativity and character.
